Winkel Tripel

  • Usage

    • Usage:*

    Winkel Tripel is a modified azimuthal projection. This projection is an arithmetic mean between two older projections; the Aitoff projection and an equi-rectangular projection (the mean applies to both x- and y-coordinates). This projection is neither equal-area nor conformal.

    *Usage information source:

    Snyder, John P. (1993) Flattening the Earth: Two Thousand Years of Map Projections. Chicago: The University of Chicago Press.
